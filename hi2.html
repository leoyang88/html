The Middle Colonies

Colonies in the Middle Region

  • New York, NY. It was originally Dutch under the name New Amsterdam, however the English seized control of it and renamed it New York.
  • Pennsylvania, PA. It was founded by William Penn who was kicked out by his father. William was a Quaker, which is basically someone who believed that everyone should be equal. Therefore, Pennsylvania was found as a place where everyone is equal.
  • New Jersey, NJ. It was found by Sir George Carteret and Lord John Berkeley. It was a reward to the two's loyalty during the English Civil War.
  • Delaware, DE. Delaware was originally founded by a Swedish company. However, William Penn seized control of Delaware for a while before it broke free.
